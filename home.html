Welcome to Carla-Coiffure

Carla-Coiffure is a hair salon in Lubumbashi, DRC, offering stylish and professional hair care for men, women, and children. We celebrate natural African hair and provide personalized, high-quality service for every client.

Our Services

  • Hair styling for men, women, and children
  • Natural African hair care and treatments
  • Hair product sales and expert advice
  • Appointment booking and consultations

Why Choose Us?

Our experienced stylists are passionate about hair care and creativity. At Carla-Coiffure, every client is treated with attention and professionalism to ensure a unique and memorable experience.
