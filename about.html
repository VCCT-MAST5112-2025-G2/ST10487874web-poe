Get to Know About Us

Carla-Coiffure is a vibrant hair salon based in Lubumbashi, DRC, dedicated to providing exceptional hairstyling services for men, women, and children. Founded in 2020, the salon combines creativity and expertise to celebrate natural African hair, offering personalized care and the latest styling trends. At Carla-Coiffure, every client is treated with passion and professionalism to ensure a unique and memorable hair care experience.

Vision

Our vision is to grow Carla-Coiffure into a leading brand that not only serves clients locally but also exports premium hair care products and supplies salons across the country and internationally. We strive to set the standard for quality, innovation, and inclusivity in the hair care industry.

Mission

At Carla-Coiffure, our mission is to provide an exceptional hair care experience for everyone, regardless of age or gender. We are dedicated to using high-quality products and professional techniques to help our clients embrace and care for their natural African hair, promoting confidence and self-expression through every hairstyle.

Our team

At Carla-Coiffure, our strength lies in our talented and passionate team. Each member is dedicated to providing exceptional hair care and creating styles that make our clients feel confident and beautiful. Stylists & Hair Experts: Skilled professionals trained in modern techniques and specialized in caring for natural African hair. They stay updated with the latest trends to offer innovative styles for all ages and genders. Customer Care Specialists: Friendly and knowledgeable team members who ensure every client receives a personalized, comfortable, and enjoyable experience. Product & Sales Team: Experts in hair care products who provide guidance on the best solutions for each client’s hair type and needs. Support Staff: Behind-the-scenes heroes who keep our salon organized, hygienic, and welcoming for everyone who walks through our doors.

Why we stand out

Our team is more than just professionals—they are passionate artists who care deeply about helping clients embrace their natural beauty. Together, we create a supportive, inclusive, and creative environment where every hairstyle is a masterpiece.
